GEARBLOOD

GearBlood is a weatherbound racing game, built from rust, memory, and the silence between engines.

It begins with a drive. A single road. One rival. A car that remembers.

You’re a field scientist with a leaking tank and a voice beside you that might be real. Or not..
Every drop of rain, every dent in the hood, every echo in the trees — it all matters.

This is not about saving the world. Just trying not to forget it.

Built for PC. Inspired by real storms and unreal silence.

In time, it will find its place on the Epic Games Store and Steam.
No microtransactions. No noise. Just the long road ahead..
